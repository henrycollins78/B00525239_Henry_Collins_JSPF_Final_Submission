What is BabylonJS?

BabylonJS is a powerful, open-source JavaScript framework that enables the creation of 3D graphics and games within the web browser. Whether you are developing a simple 3D model viewer or a complex 3D game, BabylonJS offers a variety of features such as physics, animation, and materials to bring your ideas to life.

You can find more information, tools, and tips on BabylonJS at the official website here.

Element 1 is a demonstration of the basic principles of BabylonJS: shapes, lighting, motion and textures.

Element 2 contains a simple 3D environment using similar principles to Element 1. It uses meshes and features such as a skybox to recreate a simple village scene.

Element 3 is an example of player animations and physics, which are essential to any 3D game.

Element 4 is a demonstration of Game User Interface (GUI) and audio feedback in BabylonJS.
